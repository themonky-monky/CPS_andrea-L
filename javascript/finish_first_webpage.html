Mountains of the World

Mountains are large landforms that rise high above the surrounding land. They are formed by movements of the Earth's crust and are often found in ranges. Mountains are important because they affect weather, provide homes for wildlife, and supply fresh water from melted snow. People visit mountains for hiking, skiing, and to enjoy the beautiful views.

Natural Bridges National Monument is a national monument located in southeastern Utah, USA. It was established to protect three natural rock bridges: Sipapu Bridge, Kachina Bridge, and Owachomo Bridge. The monument is known for its stunning geological formations, hiking trails, and rich cultural history.

Welcome to Natural Bridges National Monument!

Natural Bridges National Monument is not only a destination for outdoor enthusiasts, but it also offers a glimpse into the rich history of the area. The park is home to ancient petroglyphs created by the Ancestral Puebloans, offering a cultural connection to the past. Visitors can learn about these historical artifacts while exploring the park's trails, making it an enriching experience for history buffs as well as nature lovers. Whether you're photographing the dramatic arches, hiking through canyons, or simply enjoying the tranquil atmosphere, there's always something new to discover at Natural Bridges National Monument.

Top 4 Reasons to Visit Natural Bridges National Monument

  • Explore the three stunning natural bridges: Sipapu, Kachina, and Owachomo.
  • Hike scenic trails through ancient landscapes and canyons.
  • Experience star-gazing in one of the darkest skies in the country.
  • Enjoy a peaceful, less crowded park perfect for solitude and reflection.
